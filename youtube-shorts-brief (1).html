03 — Series Formats

Each Short must have a hook in 0–2 seconds. Pick one of these four proven structures and rotate between them to prevent fatigue:

01
"This AI tool does X in 30 seconds"

Pure demo format. Show the problem, show the solution, show the result. No fluff. Insane watch time retention.

02
"I replaced [software] with AI — here's what happened"

Controversy + transformation. Strong hook emotion: curiosity + FOMO. Comment magnets.

03
"5 AI tools freelancers are using to 10× income"

List format. Best for saves and shares. People bookmark these. High RPM from sponsorship too.

04
"Most people don't know ChatGPT can do this"

Revelation format. Triggers a "wait what?" reaction and immediate share impulse. Highest viral coefficient.

04 — Content Strategy
Dimension Recommendation
Posting cadence 5× per week minimum for first 60 days. The algorithm rewards consistency over quality initially.
Optimal length 38–52 seconds. Long enough for retention signal, short enough for rewatch loops.
Hook structure First frame = result, not setup. Show the "after" first, then explain how.
Engagement driver End with a question: "Which of these do you already use?" drives comment velocity.
Sentiment control Keep tone practical and warm — not hype, not academic. "Your smart friend explaining tools."
Monetization stack YouTube Partner Program + AI tool affiliates (high ticket) + digital product (AI prompt packs, templates).
05 — Growth Roadmap
Days 1–30
VOLUME PHASE

Post 5×/week. Test 4 formats. Don't optimize — just ship. Goal: 20 videos live, find your top 3 performers.

Days 31–60
DOUBLING DOWN

Replicate your top-performing format 3× per week. Begin thumbnail and hook A/B testing. Target 1K subscribers.

Days 61–90
MONETIZE & SCALE

Apply for YPP (1K subs / 10M Shorts views). First affiliate deal. Repurpose top Shorts into long-form for search traffic.

Month 4+
COMPOUND GROWTH

Shorts feed long-form. Long-form feeds watch time. Watch time feeds algorithm boost. Series brand identity solidifies.
